. Our Journey

It all began back in 2018, when we first got into cycling. A few months later, our father joined us and got a bike of his own. We connected with the local cycling group and began volunteering as mechanics, offering free bike repairs and installation.

A year later, our passion evolved into a business. We opened a small bike shop right in front of our house and began investing in special tools and quality parts. Today, we're known throughout our community for our expertise, honesty, and genuine passion for cycling.
